Manson Inside Your Computer

Installation Instructions

 

1.    Create a sub-folder in your Themes folder.
2.    Name the folder Manson Inside Your Computer .
3.    Unzip all of the files into that folder.
4.    Go to Desktop Themes in the control panel.
5.    Choose other, then open the Manson folder.
6.    Double-click on the Manson Theme.
7.    Hit the OK button.
