hi everyone, Welcome to my 10th successful theme

for those of you who have windows 95 Plus. 
Heres what ya do.
create a new file reading eeyore into your themes folder 
and Unzip the new theme into it.
place wtrspots.scr and MMWM32.dll into your windows/system folder
Then go into the desktop themes.  Click
on the pulldown menu and choose "eeyore".  
double click on it, and you will then
see a preview for that theme. click OK.

and enjoy
