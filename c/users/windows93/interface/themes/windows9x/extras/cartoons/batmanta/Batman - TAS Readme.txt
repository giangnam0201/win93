DESCRIPTION
------------
This theme is my tribute to one of the best cartoons ever produced!  Warner Bros, Bruce Timm and Paul Dini have done a marvelous job of adapting some of the finest super heroes ever into some of the finest cartoons ever.  They are owed a HUGE thanks! This theme contains all custom cursors (do you like the homemade coin cursor?), sounds pertaining to the villains in Arkham Asylum, a custom made wallpaper, icons I found out and about on the internet, but no screensaver at this time.

I give complete credit to Bruce Timm, Paul Dini and the creative team behind Batman:TAS and to Warner Bros. who have put out a fantastic series of cartoons. 

The only credit I take is for the creation of the cursors and putting the
theme together.  I take no credit for creating anything Batman related
except for this, and other, themes.

------------
INSTALLATION
------------
All files necessary for this Theme Pack have been compressed in a singular file.  To install this theme, simply extract all contents into your Plus! directory, or another directory of your choosing. Any .SCR files must be copied to C:\WINDOWS and all .TTF files must be copied to C:\WINDOWS\FONTS.
