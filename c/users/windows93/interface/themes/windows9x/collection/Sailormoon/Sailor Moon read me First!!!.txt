Fighting Evil by Moonlight...
Winning love by Daylight...

Welcome all you moonies out there in the Internet.

This is the first Windows 95 Desktop Theme for Sailor Moon (and the Sailor
Scouts).  All the moonies out in the Internet helped to make this theme
alive even though the show got scratched in Fall 96 here in the US.

Anyways, this theme has good stuff in it.  It is not as inventive I think
as the Gargoyles theme I made or the Olympic Theme I made but it is really 
cute.

Here is how to install it.  Open the zipped file.  Push extract.  Extract 
it to the C:\ prompt.  They will extract to their respective directories.

What this theme offers...

Wallpaper: 605X425 pixels featuring all the Sailor Senshi (Scouts).
Sounds: All the Inner Scouts.
Icons: Tuxedo Mask
Pointers: Yellow pointers with Moon Scepter .ani and Twinkle .ani
Screen Saver: Stars
Colors: Yellow and Gold
